2. Icon Anatomy

The MxTac icon mark is a geometric "M" letterform with neon glow, set within a black rounded square. Digital rain (binary + katakana) and a shield silhouette reinforce the cybersecurity domain.

Container128 x 128, rx=24, #0A0A0A
Radius ratio18.75% (scales with size)
M stroke9px, round cap + join, #00FF41
M glowGaussian blur 3px, green 40%
Shield opacity6% green overlay
Digital rainBinary + katakana, 8-12% opacity
Min size16 x 16px (favicon)

8. Usage Rules
DO
  • Use the black icon mark on light backgrounds
  • Use the neon green glow mark on dark/black backgrounds
  • Maintain clearspace of at least 1x icon height
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or distort
  • Use the wordmark variant (MxTac) for inline text references
  • Keep the digital rain at 8-15% opacity — never dominant
DON'T
  • Place the green icon on green or bright backgrounds
  • Remove or modify the neon glow filter effect
  • Rotate, tilt, or animate the logo
  • Use the logo on backgrounds lighter than #333
  • Change the M letterform or digital rain pattern
  • Use the logo smaller than 16px (favicon excepted)
